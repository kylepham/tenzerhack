DePauw Ninja

Inspired by Fruit Ninja, we created a simpler version and named it DePauw Ninja.

When the game starts, you will play through 5 rounds and guess the name of different buildings at DePauw by clicking the pictures of it.

In each round, you will be given the name of a random building, and 4 pictures of 4 different buildings are tossed. Your job is to click on the correct one before it falls off the screen.
